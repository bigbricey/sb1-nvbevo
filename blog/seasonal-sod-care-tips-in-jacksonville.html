Seasonal Sod Care Tips in Jacksonville

Jacksonville's climate offers unique challenges and opportunities for sod care. Here are some tips to keep your lawn healthy throughout the year:

Spring

As temperatures rise, it's time to start watering your sod more frequently. Consider applying a pre-emergent herbicide to prevent weeds.

Summer

Summer heat can be tough on sod. Ensure your lawn receives at least 1 inch of water per week and mow regularly to keep it healthy.

Fall

Fall is the perfect time to fertilize your sod. Aerate the soil to improve root growth and prepare your lawn for the cooler months.

Winter

Even in winter, your sod needs care. Reduce watering but keep an eye on rainfall. Avoid heavy foot traffic on dormant grass.

By following these seasonal tips, you can maintain a lush and healthy lawn all year round in Jacksonville.
